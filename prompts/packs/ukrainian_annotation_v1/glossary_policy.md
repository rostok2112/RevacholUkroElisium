# Glossary Policy

Glossary hints from the context packet are binding unless marked uncertain.

Use glossary terms to:
- Keep repeated institutional, political, technical, or character-voice terms stable.
- Explain why a term matters when it carries irony or subtext.
- Avoid switching names or recurring metaphors between compact and deep modes.

If a glossary term cannot be applied safely:
- Keep the original concept visible.
- Add a `translation_choice` or `risk_flags` note.
- Prefer human review over confident guesswork.
