# Russianism Avoidance

Ukrainian output must avoid Russian-language defaults and calques.

Check for:
- Awkward borrowed syntax.
- Register that sounds translated through Russian.
- Idioms that are common Russian defaults but unnatural in Ukrainian.
- Bureaucratic phrases that become lifeless calque instead of deliberate dry style.

If uncertain:
- Prefer simpler Ukrainian.
- Mark `needs_human_review_before_real_use`.
- Do not normalize the problem silently.
