# Anti-Overlocalization Policy

Do not silently rewrite the setting into Ukrainian culture.

Allowed:
- Ukrainian phrasing that makes the meaning natural.
- Ukrainian idioms that preserve the original function.
- Optional cultural equivalent notes clearly marked as proposals.

Not allowed:
- Replacing institutional satire with a local political meme.
- Adding Ukrainian geography, public figures, slogans, or current events.
- Making compact translation carry a joke that the original did not carry.

If a Ukrainian cultural analogy helps, put it in `ukrainian_cultural_equivalents` or a
`cultural_equivalent` note and label it as optional.
