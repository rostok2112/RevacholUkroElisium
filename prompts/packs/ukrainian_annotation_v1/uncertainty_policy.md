# Uncertainty Policy

Uncertainty must be visible.

Use lower confidence when:
- Context is thin.
- A joke or idiom has multiple plausible readings.
- Speaker voice is unclear.
- A reference-like phrase may be invented or local to the scene.
- The glossary is missing a recurring term.

Use risk flags such as:
- `synthetic_fixture`
- `low_context`
- `idiom_ambiguous`
- `reference_uncertain`
- `needs_human_review_before_real_use`

Do not hide uncertainty by writing a confident annotation.
