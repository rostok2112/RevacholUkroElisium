# Ukrainian Localization Style Guide

## Core voice

- Literary, sharp, sometimes filthy, but never generic.
- Ukrainian should carry the sentence's social temperature.
- Preserve discomfort and ambiguity.

## Idioms

Use Ukrainian idioms when they match function, not surface meaning.

Examples of policy:
- English idiom with clear Ukrainian equivalent → use Ukrainian equivalent.
- Culture-specific reference → translate literal meaning and annotate.
- Wordplay → choose between preserving joke effect and explaining in annotation.
- Meme-like Ukrainian insert → optional annotation/alternate, not default.

## Russianism filter

Flag:
- прямий калькований синтаксис,
- русизми,
- канцелярит that is not intentionally bureaucratic,
- unnatural participle-heavy phrasing,
- "по-російськи красиво, українською мертво".

## Register

- Skills may sound distinct.
- Institutions can sound bureaucratic.
- Street characters can be rough, but not all roughness is the same.
- Do not flatten everyone into one modern Ukrainian internet voice.
