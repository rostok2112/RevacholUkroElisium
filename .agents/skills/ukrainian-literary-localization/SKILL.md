# Ukrainian Literary Localization Skill

Use this skill for translation, glossary, style guide, idioms, cultural adaptation, and QA.

## Principles

- Preserve meaning and voice before prettiness.
- Ukrainian must sound natural, not calqued.
- Avoid Russianisms and Russian cultural defaults.
- Use Ukrainian idioms when they preserve the original function.
- Do not over-Ukrainianize the world of Revachol.
- Keep compact translation playable; put nuance in annotation.

## Review checklist

- Meaning preserved?
- Speaker/skill voice preserved?
- Ukrainian natural?
- Register right?
- Any Russianism?
- Any invented lore?
- Any spoiler leak?
- Is annotation useful and short?
